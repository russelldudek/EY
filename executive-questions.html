Discovery set

Ask where confidence is won or lost.

Questions designed to uncover the actual operating mandate.

Q1
Mandate
Where does a promising physical-AI workstream most often lose momentum today: simulation fidelity, physical validation, client authority, production ownership or engagement economics?

Identifies the real binding constraint rather than assuming the job description lists priorities in order.

Mandate
What has to be true for EY to describe a physical-AI engagement as industrialized rather than demonstrated?

Reveals EY's definition of operating value and completion.

Decision rights
How are technical and risk decisions divided among EY robotics engineers, industry specialists, alliance partners and client operators?

Clarifies where a manager integrates judgment without overriding specialist authority.

Decision rights
Who ultimately approves the transition from simulation to controlled physical testing?

Surfaces the evidence gate and accountable authority.

Technical system
Which evidence is currently hardest to carry across the simulation-to-reality transfer?

Targets the gap that a Reality Transfer Case may help make legible.

Technical system
How do teams treat environmental novelty, sensor degradation and release regression in their test strategy?

Tests how physical uncertainty and software change are governed.

Listening discipline

Use the answer to update the candidate thesis. Do not turn every response into a pitch. Ask one follow-up that clarifies consequence, ownership or evidence.

Client adoption

Client adoption
What client behavior tells you that the capability is trusted but not over-trusted?

Connects human authority to retained use.

Practice leverage
Which components of a physical-AI engagement are expected to become reusable assets, and which should remain client-specific?

Clarifies the practice's abstraction boundary.

Practice leverage
How do lessons from incidents or edge cases improve the twin, test suite, runbook and future engagements?

Tests whether learning compounds.

Manager scorecard
How is manager success balanced across hands-on contribution, workstream quality, people leadership, utilization, margin and business development?

Reveals the actual role allocation and potential conflicts.

What would make you say after six months that this hire made the practice easier to scale without making it less rigorous?

Invites an observable definition of success.
