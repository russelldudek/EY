Complete this review at meaningful workstream transitions. It is intentionally evidence-light: link to the actual architecture, test, telemetry, risk and operating artifacts rather than copying them into a new system.

Review the weakest evidence first

The goal is not a composite score. The weakest consequential dimension should shape the next workstream decision.

Integrity rule

Do not average away a safety, authority or run-state failure. Do not use an invented maturity score. Record the evidence, the unresolved assumption, the accountable owner and the next bounded decision.
