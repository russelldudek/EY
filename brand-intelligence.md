## Typography evidence and decision
EY's public digital identity uses a proprietary/custom condensed humanist sans character. No proprietary font files are copied or committed. The campaign uses a licensed system stack led by Arial Narrow / Aptos Narrow / Helvetica Neue with explicit hierarchy, compact spacing and strong editorial scale.

## Photography, geometry and motion
The campaign does not copy client or product imagery. It derives an original three-plane simulation / physical system / client operation composition from the role and EY's public physical AI work. Motion is a candidate-created alignment scan; the EY mark is never animated.

## Independent-candidate distinction
