That is the mandate I see beneath EY's Physical AI Engineering Consultant - Manager posting: lead technical workstreams that connect robotics, digital twins, data and ML engineering to client trust, delivery economics and reusable practice capability.

My strongest evidence is adjacent rather than identical. At Vape-Jet, I lead AI-first operating transformation in a software-enabled robotics and machine-vision environment with approximately 1,000 fielded robots. At ZeusVu, I led regulated autonomous-drone operations with a 100% safety record and no FAA incidents and developed TensorFlow-based computer-vision concepts. At Compunetics, I led high-reliability engineering, manufacturing, quality systems and complex technical programs. At Amazon, I built operating mechanisms for a 24/7 Prime launch supporting approximately five million second-day orders annually. Through DudeWorth, I design AI-readiness and agentic-workflow systems that connect value, data, human judgment, governance, evaluation and adoption.
